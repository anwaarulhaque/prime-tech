Moku:Lab iPad App

Take control of your experiment with Moku:Lab’s iPad App.

Freedom to move. Freedom to measure.

Moku:Lab’s hardware and iPad app connect to each other using WiFi, allowing you to record measurements and control your experiment from anywhere in the lab. An iPad will be included with each purchase of Moku:Lab.

Intuitive user interfaces

Designed specifically for each instrument, Moku:Lab’s iPad App elevates user experience in test and measurement. Enjoy a streamlined workflow to maximize productivity. All twelve instruments share common elements and integrations that translate into a more familiar, intuitive and consistent experience in the lab no matter which instrument you need to use.

Spend more time measuring and less time fiddling

Quickly configure your instrument and get measuring sooner using the iPad’s multi-touch gesture capabilities. Adjust important parameters using intuitive gestures you’re already familiar with on a smart phone.

ILights on. Lights off.

Work in a light sensitive environment? Simply switch to dark mode.

Exporting data has never been easier

Integration with Dropbox, iCloud and MyFiles makes sharing your data easier than ever before. And if the cloud’s not your thing, you can also export your data to SD Card or share it via E-mail.

Moku:Lab gets better over time

Moku:Lab receives free over-the-air software updates, bringing new features to existing instruments, and giving you access to entirely new instruments. When you buy a Moku:Lab, it’s just the beginning. To update your Moku:Lab hardware, simply update the iPad app whenever a new release becomes available. If you have purchased Moku:Lab’s Full-suite Bundle, you will automatically gain access to each new instrument as it is released through software updates. Otherwise, you can individually purchase new instruments by selecting “Upgrade your Moku:Lab” in the store.
