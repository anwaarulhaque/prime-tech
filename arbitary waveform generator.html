Arbitrary Waveform Generator

Maximum update rate 1 GS/s
Independent channels 2
Independent triggering Burst / pulsed
DAC resolution 16-bits

Moku:Lab’s Arbitrary Waveform Generator can generate custom waveforms with up to 65,536 points at sample rates of up to 1 GSa/s. Waveforms can be loaded from a file, r input as a piece-wise mathematical function with up to 32 segments, enabling you to enerate truly arbitrary waveforms. In pulsed mode, waveforms can be output with more than 250,000 cycles of dead time between pulses, allowing you to excite your system with an arbitrary waveform at regular intervals over extended periods of time.
